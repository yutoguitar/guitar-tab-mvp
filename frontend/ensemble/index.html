About

The Ensemble

Yuto Kanazawa leads a full jazz ensemble built around the Woodsiders Collective — a NYC-based group of horn players and rhythm section musicians who bring a modern, polished sound to hotel ballrooms, corporate galas, and private events.

Configurable from quartet to octet depending on the room, with a deep book spanning swing, bebop, Latin jazz, modern jazz, and pop arrangements.

  • Swing
  • Bebop
  • Modern Jazz
  • Latin Jazz
  • Big-band Pop
  • Ballads
