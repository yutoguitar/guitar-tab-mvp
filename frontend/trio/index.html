About

The Trio

Yuto Kanazawa’s Jazz Guitar Trio pairs guitar with upright bass and drums for a classic, polished trio sound — built for NYC hotel lobbies, rooftop lounges, private dinners, and corporate events.

The set moves from the Great American Songbook through bossa nova and Latin jazz, with room for tasteful solos and crowd favorites.

  • Jazz Standards
  • Bossa Nova
  • Latin Jazz
  • Swing
  • Ballads
  • Pop Jazz
