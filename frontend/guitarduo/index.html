About

Meet the duo

Two New York-based musicians bringing the warmth and energy of Latin jazz to every event.

Yuto Kanazawa

Guitar

Born in Tokyo and trained at Berklee College of Music, Yuto has spent 20 years perfecting a sound that blends Japanese sensitivity with the warmth of Latin and Spanish guitar.

Now based in NYC, he performs weekly at the Marriott, and his solo arrangement of Sabor a Mi has been streamed over a million times on Spotify.

Ilya Dynov

Drums · Cajon · Percussion

Internationally acclaimed NYC-based drummer, vibraphonist and percussionist — praised by international press as “virtuoso,” “fantastic,” and “astonishing.” His debut album Bridges (2023) features Grammy-winner Lonnie Plaxico on bass.

Ilya has performed at major festivals across Europe and at NYC jazz clubs including Smalls and 55Bar, and plays alongside Yuto in the Woodsiders Collective and MicroCorgi. Russian/Belgian by origin, he holds a Masters from the Royal Conservatory of Antwerp.
