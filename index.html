Yuto Kanazawa (guitar) and Ilya Dynov (drums, cajon, percussion) lead Evergold Entertainment — a NYC-based booking collective offering three distinct configurations for hotels, lounges, and private events. From an intimate guitar & cajon duo to a full jazz ensemble featuring the Woodsiders Collective, every act is built from the same core of musicianship: Berklee-trained, internationally acclaimed, and heard weekly at the Marriott.
